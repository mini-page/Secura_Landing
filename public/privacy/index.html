Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 7, 2026

Secura ("Application", "we", "us") is a privacy-first mobile application developed to provide local file encryption. This Privacy Policy discloses our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information we receive through the Application.

1. Information Access and Collection

We follow a strict "data minimization" protocol. Secura accesses the following Google User Data:

  • Google Account Information: Email address and profile name (obtained via Google Sign-In) to establish your unique identity for vault access.
  • Google Drive AppData: Access to the drive.appdata scope to manage a private, hidden configuration file.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your Google data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To securely sign you into the application.
  • Cloud Sync (Settings Only): To store and restore your application preferences (e.g., theme mode, security settings) across devices.
  • Service Improvement: To improve user-facing features directly visible within the application interface.

Google API Disclosure (Limited Use)

Secura's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose your Google user data to any third parties. Specifically:

  • No Data Sale: We never sell your personal information to data brokers or advertisers.
  • No Advertising: Your data is never used for personalized or retargeted advertising.
  • No AI Training: Your data is not used to train machine learning or artificial intelligence models.
  • Third-Party Transfers: We do not transfer your data to third parties unless required for the direct functionality of the app (e.g., communicating directly with Google APIs) or for legal compliance.

Data Retention, Security, and Deletion

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security procedures to protect your data. All file encryption (AES-256-GCM) happens locally on your device. Google account metadata is protected during transit via HTTPS/TLS.

Retention Policy

We retain your Google account metadata and configuration backups only for as long as your account is active. If the application is unused for an extended period, or if you request deletion, data is removed from our local caches and your private Google Drive AppData folder.

User Control & Deletion Request

You have full control over your data:

  • In-App Deletion: You can use the "Factory Reset" option in Settings to wipe all local data.
  • Cloud Deletion: You can disconnect Secura from your Google Account via your Google Security Settings, which will prevent further access to your Drive.
  • Manual Deletion Request: To request permanent deletion of any metadata associated with your email, please contact us at the address listed in the Contact section. We will process your request within 30 days.
CRITICAL DATA LOSS WARNING: Secura is a "Local-First" application. We do not store your encrypted files on our servers. Uninstalling the Application without exporting your files will result in permanent and unrecoverable data loss. We cannot recover these files for you.
