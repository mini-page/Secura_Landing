Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 7, 2026

Secura ("we", "us", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection

Secura follows a minimal data collection principle:

  • Authentication: We use Google Sign-In to verify your identity. We only store your email address and display name locally and in a hidden backup file.
  • Usage Data: We do not track your activity, your files, or your location. All logs are stored strictly on your device for your own auditing purposes.

2. Google Drive Permissions (App Data Folder)

Secura requests access to the drive.appdata scope. This is a private, hidden folder on your Google Drive that only Secura can see.

  • What is stored: A small JSON file containing your app settings (theme, security toggles) and your profile name.
  • What is NOT stored: Your encrypted files, your vault keys, or your PIN. We never upload your actual vault data to the cloud.
  • Purpose: This allows you to restore your settings and profile when you log in on a new device.

3. Encryption & Local Storage

All files you import into Secura are encrypted using AES-256-GCM encryption. The encryption keys are derived from your PIN using zero-knowledge protocols. We never have access to your PIN or your decrypted files.

Data Handling Transparency

Critical Warning: Secura is a "Local-First" application. We do not maintain a central server. If you uninstall the app without exporting your files, your data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered by us.

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

We use a zero-knowledge security model. This means:

  • Your PIN is never sent to any server.
  • Your encryption keys are stored only in your device's hardware-backed Secure Storage.
  • If you forget your PIN and your Security Question answer, we cannot help you recover your files.
